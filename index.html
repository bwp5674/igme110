The added convenience of voice allows people to interface with devices in many contexts that were previously more difficult, or impossible. As voice interfaces improve, voice-based applications will become as intuitive as a conversation, making them more friendly and approachable for the general consumer.

As developers, we have the ability to accelerate this process by building “voice-optimized” apps for these assistants. Similar to the introduction of the smartphone, there is currently a huge market for third-party, voice-first applications.
